Introduction

A number of online retailers offer what are called affiliate programmes. An affiliate programme is an agreement in which a business such as Amazon pays you ('the affiliate') a commission for sending traffic from your website to theirs that results in the purchase of a product or service.

For more information, see:

About hyperlinks to Amazon products

On your Store page, click on either the image or the 'Buy now' button for each of the four books. You will be redirected to the following web addresses on the Amazon Ireland website:

  • https://www.amazon.ie/dp/1925836363
  • https://www.amazon.ie/dp/1849693188
  • https://www.amazon.ie/dp/0648053709
  • https://www.amazon.ie/dp/1706664680

The 10-digit number at the final part of each hyperlink is called the ASIN. It is short for Amazon.com Standard Item Number. Every book or other product offered by Amazon has a unique ASIN.

The general format of a product link to an Amazon product is therefore:

  • https://www.amazon.ie/dp/ASIN

You can find an item's ASIN on its product page under Product Details, along with other information such as sales rank and release date. You can also see the ASIN as part of the item's web address.

Amazon operates multiple online stores across different countries. Each country store will have a different top-level-domain (TLD). For example:

  • amazon.com
  • amazon.co.uk
  • amazon.de
  • amazon.fr

For this project, use whichever country store you typically choose for making purchases from Amazon.

About your Amazon ID

When you join Amazon's reseller programme, you will be provided with a unique ID. Different country-based stores use a different term for this ID. Typically, there are:

  • AssociatesID
  • AffiliateID
  • StoreID

You include this unique ID in your hyperlink to an Amazon product. See the example below.

  • https://www.amazon.com/dp/ASIN/ref=nosim?tag=ID

If a visitor to your web page clicks on such a hyperlink and then purchases this or another product, Amazon will provide you with a commission on that sale.

Join the Amazon reseller programme

Your next task is to join the Amazon reseller programme. Here are the steps:

  1. Go to the home page of Amazon country website you typically use. For example, amazon.ie.
  2. Scroll down to the footer and click the Associates Programme link. This link may also be called Become an Affiliate.
  3. You will see a web page similar to that below. Click the yellow Sign up button.
  4. Enter your Email and chosen Password. If you already have a customer account with Amazon, you can reuse the same Email and Password details.
  5. Enter further details as required for Amazon to generate your unique reseller ID.

Your reseller ID

As part of your registration, you will be asked to enter an Amazon Associates ID/AffiliateID/StoreID.

For example, if your name is Mary Smith, you could enter msmith.

Amazon will then generate a unique ID for you such as msmith-1234.

Update the product links on your Store page
