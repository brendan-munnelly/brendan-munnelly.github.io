Moving forward: further learning and certification resources

A selection of learning resources I would recommend to further your career in the digital world.

Web Design

I strongly recommend checking out the online Responsive Web Design course from FreeCodeCamp and its learner-friendly Community Forum.

Moreover, FreeCodeCamp offers a recognised certification (also for free!) that will look impressive on your CV or Linkedin profile.

You have already covered about 80% of the material in this course, so you should be able to complete it in a few weeks.

Graphic Design

Want to learn about Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop? Then this is the course and certification for you:

IBAT Graphic Design & Desktop Publishing Diploma

This part-time course concentrates on the fundamentals of the above Adobe platforms essential for people in creative, marketing and communications roles.

Full Stack Development Diploma

If you want to move forward into developing web and mobile apps, then you will be interested in this course:

Full Stack Development Diploma

Coursera and Edx

These are two (rival) networks of mostly US universities that offer a wide range of free online courses. However, you need to pay for the certification.

Here are a few examples:

  • Programming with JavaScript (Coursera)
  • Foundations of Digital Marketing and E-commerce (Coursera)
  • CS50's Web Programming with Python and JavaScript (edX)
  • Introduction to Web Development with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (edX)

Here is another entry-level digital marketing course I would recommend:

Digital Marketing Course (HubSpot Academy)
